## Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

TierQuest does not collect personal data, use third-party analytics, or show ads.

The information you enter in the app is stored locally on your device and is used only to provide the app’s features. If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. TierQuest does not receive your full payment details.

If this changes, this policy will be updated here.
